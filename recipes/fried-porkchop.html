Fried Porkchop Recipe

Description

Homemade Fried Porkchop isn't as hard to make as it seems. Don't be intimidated by all the layers of delicious Italian flavor â€” try this homemade porkchop recipe today!

Ingredients

  • 1kg pork chops
  • Breading
  • Bread crumbs
  • Basil, oregano, salt, black pepper
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Season meat with spices
  2. Coat in breading
  3. Dip in egg
  4. Coat in breadcrumbs
  5. Fry until golden brown
